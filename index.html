Analyzing Energy Efficiency Alternatives

Purdue WALC

As part of my Systems Engineering Design course, our team evaluated rooftop solar, wind, and piezometric tiles for a 24-hour library using discrete optimization and lifecycle economics. Our final design recommended rooftop solar panels with a Benefit-Cost Ratio = 2.57 and projected 20-year savings through reduced energy cost and incentives, annual energy costs, and potential green incentives!
